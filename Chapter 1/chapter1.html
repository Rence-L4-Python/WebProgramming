Egg Fried Rice

The Egg Fried Rice is a dish that works best in the early morning after a particularly heavy night of festivity or an abundance of cooked meals. Originating from China, the Egg Fried Rice dish is a popular dish with unique renditions from neighboring countries in Asia -- this recipe being a Filipino-styled variant.

(This recipe is originally from Panlasang Pinoy.)

Ingredients

  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of sugar
  • 1/2 chopped bell pepper
  • 3/4 cup of chopped green onions
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1/4 teaspoon of sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 3 chopped sweet peppers
  • 4 eggs
  • 4 cups of leftover rice
  • 4 tablespoons of cooking oil

How to cook Egg Fried Rice

  1. Crack the 4 eggs and place in a bowl.
  2. Pour all the cooking oil in a wok and heat it. Cook until the sides on the pan turn brown, then flip them over and repeat for the other side.
  3. Add the bell peppers and sweet peppers. Saut√© them both for 1 minute as you break the eggs apart.
  4. Add 2 cups of the leftover rice. Stir fry for 2 minutes.
  5. Add the final 2 cups of leftover rice. Continue cooking until all ingredients are well blended.
  6. Pour the sesame oil and soy sauce. Continue stir frying for 2 minutes.
  7. Season with salt and sugar, and add the green onions. Toss until well blended.
  8. Your dish is finished! Transfer to your plates and eat well.
